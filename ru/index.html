Ameer-ul-Momineen, jin ki hikmat dilon ko munawwar karti hai aur jin ka adl tamaam insaniyat ke liye abadi miyaar hai

Sher-e-Khuda, Bab-ul-Ilm

Hazrat Ali Ibn Abi Talib (Razi Allahu Anhu) (600–661 Isvi) Nabi Kareem Sallallahu Alaihi Wasallam ke chacha zaad bhai aur damaad thay, jinhe bachpan se hi Nabi Kareem ﷺ ke ghar mein paala gaya. Woh sabse pehle Islam qubool karne walon mein se thay aur unhon ne apni poori zindagi Islam ki khidmat ke liye waqf kar di.

Ahl-e-Sunnat mein unhe Chauthe Khalifa Rashid aur Ahl-e-Tashayyu mein Pehle Imam ke taur par izzat di jaati hai — ek aisi shakhsiyat jin ka maqaam Islami saqafat mein Nabi Kareem ﷺ ke baad sab se buland maana jaata hai.

Apne laqab Mushkil Kusha (mushkilaat ko hal karne wale) se mashhoor, laakhon log rozana unka naam le kar mushkilaat mein unki shafa'at talab karte hain. Adl, ilm, bahaduri aur roohani gehrai ki unki miras tamaam hudood se mawara insaniyat ko muta'assir karti rehti hai.

Ahl-e-Bait — Nabi ﷺ Ka Gharana

Hazrat Ali (RA) Banu Hashim mein paida hue — Quraish ka sab se mo'azzaz qabeela. Unke waalid Abu Talib qabeele ke sardaar aur Nabi Kareem ﷺ ke mehboob chacha aur muhafiz thay.

Nabi ﷺ ki mehboob sahebzadi Fatima al-Zahra (RA) se unki shaadi ne Islam ke do muqaddas tareen khandaanon ko jod diya. Is mubarak rishte se Hasan aur Husain — Nabi ﷺ ke mehboob nawaase — aur sahebzaadiyan Zainab aur Umm Kulthum paida huin.

Hazrat Ali (RA) ki aulaad, jinhe Saadaat aur Shurafa kaha jaata hai, ne poori Islami taarikh mein aham kirdaar ada kiya hai aur aaj bhi poori Muslim duniya mein qadr ki nigaah se dekhe jaate hain.

Hadith-e-Kisa

"Yeh mere Ahl-e-Bait hain. Ae Allah, in se har najaasat door farma aur inhe khoob paak farma."

— Nabi Muhammad ﷺ (Sahih Muslim)

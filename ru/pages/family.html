اَہلِ بَیت

Khandaan wa Nasab

Hazrat Ali (RA) ka ba-barkat khandaan — Khana-e-Abu Talib se Ahl-e-Bayt tak

إِنَّمَا يُرِيدُ اللَّهُ لِيُذْهِبَ عَنكُمُ الرِّجْسَ أَهْلَ الْبَيْتِ وَيُطَهِّرَكُمْ تَطْهِيرًا

"Allah sirf yeh chahta hai ke Ae Ahl-e-Bayt, tum se har napaaki door kar de aur tumhen poori tarah paak kar de."

— Quran 33:33 (Ayat-e-Tatheer)

Walidain Ka Nasab

WALID

Abu Talib bin Abdul Muttalib

أَبُو طَالِب بن عَبْدِ الْمُطَّلِب

Abu Talib Banu Hashim qabeelay ke sardaar aur Nabi Kareem ﷺ ke chacha aur sarparast the. Jab Aap ﷺ ke dada Abdul Muttalib ka intiqal hua to Abu Talib ne nauumr Muhammad ﷺ ko apne bete ki tarah paala. Woh Makkah mein sakht tareen eeza rasaani ke dauran bhi Nabi ﷺ ke saabit qadam muhafiz rahe. Unka ghar imaan, sharafat aur sakhawat ka ghar tha.

WALIDA

Fatima bint Asad

فَاطِمَة بِنْت أَسَد

Fatima bint Asad ibtidai Muslimanon mein se theen aur apne waqt ki muazzaz tareen khawateen mein shumaar hoti theen. Jab Nabi ﷺ yateem hue to unhone Aap ﷺ ko apne bete ki tarah paala aur itni mohabbat se dekha ke Aap ﷺ khud unhen "meri maa" kehte the. Jab unka intiqal hua to Nabi ﷺ ne apna kurta unki qabr mein rakha — yeh ghair-mamooli mohabbat aur izzat ka amal tha.

Mubarak Nikaah

AHLIYA

Fatima Zahra (SA)

فَاطِمَة الزَّهْرَاء

Fatima Zahra (SA), Nabi Kareem ﷺ aur Khadija (RA) ki mahboob sahabzadi, ko ilaahi hukm se Hazrat Ali ke nikaah mein diya gaya. Nabi ﷺ ne farmaya: "Agar Ali na hote to Fatima ka koi hamsar na tha."

Unka nikaah Islami tareekh ka sabse muqaddas rishta maana jaata hai. Intehai saadgi mein rehne ke bawajood — unke ghar mein ek chatai, khajoor ke reshon se bhara takiya aur mashkeeza ke siwa kuch na tha — unka ghar imaan, mohabbat aur roohani noor se roshan tha. Woh Hasan, Husayn, Zainab aur Umm Kulthum ke walidain hain — woh mubarak nasl jis ke zariye Nabi ﷺ ka silsila-e-nasab jaari hai.

Fatima ko kai alqabaat se nawaaza gaya: al-Zahra (Noorani), al-Batool (Paakdaman), Sayyidat Nisa al-Alamin (Jahan ki Khawateen ki Sardaar), aur Umm Abiha (Apne Walid ki Maa).

Ali Aur Fatima Ki Aulaad

Imam Hasan (RA)

الحَسَن

Bade sahabzaade, sakhawat aur hikmat ke liye mashoor. Mukhtasar muddat ke liye paanchwen Khalifa bane aur Muslim ummat mein mazeed khoonreezi rokne ke liye Muawiyah se muahida-e-sulah kiya. Unke naam ka matlab "Bhala" ya "Haseen Soorat" hai. Shakl-o-soorat aur akhlaq mein Nabi ﷺ se sabse zyada mushaabih the.

Imam Husayn (RA)

الحُسَيْن

Sayyid-ush-Shuhada. 680 Iswi mein Karbala ka unka bahadurana mauqif — jab sirf 72 saathiyon ke saath hazaaron ki fauj ka saamna kiya — insaani tareekh ki azeem tareen qurbaniyon mein se ek hai. Nabi ﷺ ne farmaya: "Husayn mujh se hai aur main Husayn se hoon. Allah us se mohabbat karta hai jo Husayn se mohabbat kare."

Zainab bint Ali (RA)

زَيْنَب

Sabr aur bahaduri ka mujassama. Karbala ke saanhe ke baad unhone Husayn ka paighaam duniya tak pahunchaya. Ibn Ziyad aur Yazeed ke darbaar mein unke josheele khutbaat ne zulm ko be-naqab kiya aur haq ko mahfooz kiya. Woh "Karbala ki Heroine" ke naam se mashoor hain.

Umm Kulthum bint Ali (RA)

أُمّ كُلْثُوم

Apni behan Zainab ki tarah unhone bhi Karbala ke saanhe ko be-panaah bahaduri se bardaasht kiya. Taqwa, fasaahat aur sabr ke liye mashoor theen aur Karbala aur uske baad ke waaqiaat ki gawaahi dene mein Zainab ke shaana ba-shaana khadi raheen.

Shareef Nasab

QABEELA

Quraysh Ke Banu Hashim

Hazrat Ali ibn Abi Talib ka taaluq Banu Hashim se tha, jo Quraysh qabeelay ka sabse muazzaz aur ba-waqaar khandaan tha — wohi khandaan jis se Nabi Kareem ﷺ taaluq rakhte the. Unka silsila-e-nasab yun hai:

  • Ali bin Abu Talib bin Abdul Muttalib
  • Abdul Muttalib bin Hashim (Banu Hashim ke baani)
  • Hashim bin Abd Manaf
  • Yeh silsila Adnan aur bil-aakhir Hazrat Ibrahim (AS) tak jaata hai

Is se Hazrat Ali Nabi ﷺ ke haqeeqi chacha-zaad bhai theharte hain — Abu Talib Nabi ﷺ ke chacha aur sarparast the. Hazrat Ali kam umri se Nabi ﷺ ke ghar mein pale badhe jis se unke darmiyan rishta mazeed mazboot hua.

Ahl-e-Bayt — Gharana-e-Nabuwwat

Nabawi Khandaan

Ahl-e-Bayt se muraad Nabi Kareem ﷺ ka khandaan hai. Agarcheh iski tareef ke baare mein ulama mein bahas hai, lekin sabse maqbool riwaayat yeh hai ke Nabi ﷺ ne Ali, Fatima, Hasan aur Husayn ko apni chaadar mein jama kiya (waaqi'a-e-Kisa) aur dua farmaai:

"Ae Allah! Yeh mere Ahl-e-Bayt hain. In se har napaaki door kar de aur inhen mukammal taur par paak kar de."

— Nabi Kareem ﷺ (Hadees-e-Kisa, Sahih Muslim)

Ahl-e-Bayt se mohabbat har Musalmaan par farz samjhi jaati hai. Quran farmata hai: "Kaho: Main tum se apne qarabat daaron ki mohabbat ke siwa koi ajr nahi maangta" (42:23). Hazrat Ali aur Fatima ke zariye Nabi ﷺ ka mubarak silsila-e-nasab jaari hai — Saadaat aur Shurafa jo aaj is azeem khandaan se nisbat rakhte hain duniya bhar mein laakhhon karoron ki tadaad mein hain.

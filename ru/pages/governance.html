Hukumrani wa Adl

Qiyadat ka aisa namoona jo itna aadilana aur itna shafqat aamez tha ke tareekh mein is ki misaal nahi milti

Khilafat-e-Ali (656–661 Iswi)

Jab Hazrat Usman (RA) ki shahadat ke baad Hazrat Ali ne khilafat sambhali to unhen ek bikhri hui Muslim ummat mili. Siyasi bohran ke bawajood unhone adl par koi samjhauta karne se inkaar kar diya aur haq ki qeemat par siyasi maslahit ikhtiyar nahi ki. Unki khilafat — agarcheh siyasi halchal se bhari thi — akhlaqi hukumrani ka sunehri miyaar qaim kar gayi.

Unhone darul-khilafa Madina se Kufa (maujuda Iraq) muntaqil kiya aur ek aisi hukumat qaim ki jo musawaat, shaffafiyat aur muashre ke sabse kamzor afraad ki khidmat ke usoolon par mabni thi.

Hukumrani Ke Bunyadi Usool

⚖️

Mukammal Adl

Apne upar, apne khandan aur sabse ghareeb shehri par ek hi qanoon naafiz kiya. Koi bhi qanoon se baala nahi tha.

🤝

Musaawi Huqooq

Ghair Muslimon ko Muslimanon jaise huqooq haasil the. Esaai, Yahoodi aur Zartushthi mahfooz shehri the.

🏛️

Bait-ul-Maal Ki Amanat

Bait-ul-Maal se ek dirham bhi zyada lene se inkaar kiya. Daulat baraabar taqseem ki.

🕊️

Shafqat

Zaati taur par raat ko galiyon mein nikalte the taake gharebon, yateemon aur budhon ka haal jaan sakein.

📜

Shaffafiyat

Tamam hukumati faislay awami satah par kiye. Jaanch-partaal aur mashwarat ki dawat di.

🛡️

Zaati Saadgi

Apne kapde khud seete the, jau ki roti khate the, aur mitti ke saada ghar mein rehte the — halanke ek saltanat par hukumat kar rahe the.

Adl Ki Mashoor Misaalein

⚔️

Unki Zireh Aur Yahoodi Shakhs

Hazrat Ali ki zireh gum ho gayi aur baad mein ek Yahoodi shakhs ke paas mili. Unhone muqaddama qazi ke saamne pesh kiya. Qazi ne saboot maanga, aur jab Hazrat Ali sirf apne bete Hasan ko gawaah ke taur par pesh kar sake to qazi ne ise radd kar diya (kyunke baap ke haq mein bete ki gawaahi qabool nahi thi). Hazrat Ali muqaddama haar gaye.

Yahoodi shakhs is hukumraan ke adl se itna mutassir hua jis ne apni hi adalat ke faislay ko qabool kiya, ke usne kaha: "Yeh Ambiyaa ka adl hai." Phir usne apni marzi se Islam qabool kar liya aur zireh waapis kar di, aitiraaf karte hue ke woh waaqi usi ne li thi.

🍞

Raat Ki Sair

Hazrat Ali raat ko bhes badal kar Kufa ki galiyon mein nikalte the taake apni raaya ka haal dekh sakein. Ek baar unhen ek aurat mili jis ke bhuke bachche ro rahe the — woh haandi mein patthar ubaal rahi thi taake bachchon ko tasalli ho ke khaana pak raha hai. Hazrat Ali fauran Bait-ul-Maal gaye, khud apni peeth par khaana utha kar laaye, khud khaana pakaya aur bachchon ko khilaya.

Jab unke saathi ne khaana uthane ki peshkash ki to Hazrat Ali ne farmaya: "Kya tum Qayamat ke din bhi mera bojh uthaoge?"

💰

Daulat Ki Musaawi Taqseem

Jab Bait-ul-Maal mein raqam aati to Hazrat Ali ise baraabar taqseem karte — har Musalman ko yeksaan hissa milta, chahe samaaji haisiyat, qabeela ya nasl kuch bhi ho. Unhone Quraysh ko doosre Arabon par, ya Arabon ko ghair Arabon par fauqiyat dene se inkaar kar diya. Yeh bunyadi musawaat un taaqatwar khandaanon ki mukhalifat ki ek wajah thi jo pichli hukumaton mein tarjeehi salook se faida utha rahe the.

📜 Malik al-Ashtar Ko Khat — Hukumrani Ka Manshoor

Jab Hazrat Ali ne Malik al-Ashtar ko Misr ka Governor muqarrar kiya to unhone ek tafseeli khat likha jise "Islami tareekh mein hukumrani ki behtareen dastaweez" kaha gaya hai — aur shayad pehli sadi Iswi mein siyasat par likhi jaane wali sabse aham tahreer hai.

Yeh khat hukumrani ke har pehlu ka ehaata karta hai: uhdeydaaron ka intikhab, aqalliyaton ke saath salook, tax, adalat, fauj, tijaarat, gharebon ki falah, aur hukumraan ka apna tarz-e-amal. Ise United Nations ne insaani huqooq ke usoolon ki ek qaabil-e-zikr ibtidai dastaweez tasleem kiya hai.

"Unke saamne aisa darinda mat bano jo unhen aasan shikaar samjhe, kyunke woh do qisam ke hain: ya to deen mein tumhare bhai hain ya takhleeq mein tumhare jaise. Galtiyan un se naagahaan ho jaati hain, kamzoriyan un par ghaalib aa jaati hain, aur bure kaam un se jaan bujh kar ya bhool se sarzad hote hain. Pas unke saath apni maafi aur darguzar isi tarah rakho jaise tum chahte ho ke Khuda tumhen maaf kare aur darguzar kare."

— Khat 53, Nahj al-Balagha (Malik al-Ashtar ko khat)

"Apne logon mein se behtareen ko adalat ke liye chuno, aisa jo mushkil muqaddamaat se maghloob na ho, tanazaat se naaraaz na ho, galti par qaim na rahe, haq talaash karne mein hichkichahat na ho, laalchi na ho, sathi samajh par mutmainn na ho, shukook mein sabse zyada rukne wala ho, daleel par sabse zyada bharosa karne wala ho, fareeqain ki faryaad se sabse kam thakne wala ho, maamlaat ki tehqeeq mein sabse zyada sabr wala ho."

— Qaziyon ke intikhab par, Khat 53

"Sabse nichla tabqa zarooratmandon aur miskeen logon ka hai. Khuda ne unke huqooq hukumranon par laazim kiye hain. Pas unke liye Bait-ul-Maal se hissa muqarrar karo aur fatah shudah zameenon ki paidawaar se hissa muqarrar karo... kyunke un mein sabse door wala bhi usi ka mustahiq hai jis ka qareeb wala hai."

— Falah aur samaaji insaaf par, Khat 53

🌍 Deerpa Asraat

Hazrat Ali ke nizam-e-hukumrani ne in shaubon ko mutassir kiya:

  • Islami Fiqh — unki qanooni aaraa tamam Islami fiqhi makateeb-e-fikr ki bunyaad hain
  • Siyasi Falsafa — unka tasawwur ke hukumraan awaam ka khaadim hai, daur-e-roshni khayali se sadiyon pehle ka hai
  • Insaani Huqooq — Malik al-Ashtar ko unka khat jadeed insaani huqooq ki dastawezaat se ek hazaar saal pehle likha gaya
  • Sufiyaana Akhlaaqiyaat — zaati saadgi aur khidmat par unki taakeed Sufi riwaayaat ka markaz bani
  • Jadeed Hukumrani — ulama aur rehnamaa aaj bhi unke usoolon ka mutala jadeed hukumat mein amal daraamad ke liye karte hain

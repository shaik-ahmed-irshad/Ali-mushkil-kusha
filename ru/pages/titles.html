الْقَابُ وَ صِفَات

Ilahi Alqaabaat wa Sifaat

Hazrat Ali (RA) ka har laqab aap ki ghair-maamooli shakhsiyat ke ek pehlu ko roshan karta hai — ilahi bahaaduri se le kar bepanah hikmat tak

✨

مُشْکِل کُشَا

Mushkil Kusha

Mushkilein Hal Karne Waale

Maani wa Ahmiyat

Aap ke tamaam alqaabaat mein sab se mahboob laqab Mushkil Kusha hai jis ka matlab hai "Mushkilein Hal Karne Waale" ya "Musibatein Door Karne Waale." Duniya bhar ke laakhon Musalman mushkil ke waqt is naam ko pukaarte hain aur Hazrat Ali (RA) ki roohaani shafaa'at ke zariye sukoon haasil karte hain.

Yeh laqab is gehre emaan ki akkaasi karta hai ke Allah aur Uske Rasool ﷺ se qurbat ki wajah se Ali ki roohaani taaqat aaj bhi un logon ko tasalli aur hal faraaham karti hai jo sacche dil se pukaarte hain. "Ya Ali Mushkil Kusha" ki tilaawat South Asia, Central Asia, Middle East aur is se aage wasee paimaane par raaij hai.

"Ali ko pukaaro, ajaibaat ke mazhar ko. Tum use mushkilaat mein madadgaar paoge. Har gham aur ranj door ho jaayega."

— Naad-e-Ali (Aalam-e-Islam mein wasee paimaane par padhi jaane waali dua)

🦁

اَسَدُ اللّٰہ

Asadullah / Sher-e-Khuda

Khuda Ka Sher

Maani wa Ahmiyat

Asadullah (Arabi) aur Sher-e-Khuda (Urdu/Farsi) dono ka tarjuma "Khuda Ka Sher" hai. Yeh laqab Ali ki ghair-maamooli jismaani bahaaduri aur har jang mein Islam ke be-khauf difaa ki izzat afzaai karta hai.

Aap ne kabhi kisi jang se peechhe nahi hate, har muqaable mein har hareef ko shikast di, aur apni be-misaal taaqat ke liye mashoor the — ba-shamool Khaybar ke qile ka darwaaza ukhaadhne ka afsaanwi kaamaama. Aap ki bahaaduri is qadar mashoor thi ke dushmanon ne bhi aap ko apne daur ka sab se bada jangju tasleem kiya.

"Koi bahaadur jawaan nahi siwaaye Ali ke, aur koi talwar nahi siwaaye Zulfiqar ke."

— لَا فَتَىٰ إِلَّا عَلِيٌّ لَا سَيْفَ إِلَّا ذُو الْفِقَار (Jang-e-Uhud ke dauraan ailaan hua)

📖

بَابُ الْعِلْم

Bab-ul-Ilm

Ilm Ka Darwaaza

Maani wa Ahmiyat

Nabi Kareem ﷺ ne khud yeh laqab Ali ko ata farmaya aur unhein woh darwaaza qaraar diya jis se emaan, shariat aur roohaaniyat ka ilm behta hai. Ali ko tamaam Sahaba mein sab se zyada ilm waala samjha jaata tha — Tafseer-e-Quran, Fiqh, Ilm-e-Kalaam, Arabi Grammar aur Fasaahat wa Balaaaghat ke maahir.

Aap ki ilmi miraas Nahj al-Balagha (Fasaahat Ki Choti) mein mehfooz hai jis mein khutbaat, khutoot aur aqwaal hain jo aalami adab ke azeem tareen shaahkaaron ka muqaabla karte hain. Aap ke zamaane ke sab se bade ulama bhi aap ke faislay ki pairwi karte aur aap se mashwara talab karte the.

"Main ilm ka sheher hoon aur Ali uska darwaaza hain. Jo sheher mein daakhil hona chaahe woh darwaaze se aaye."

— Nabi Kareem ﷺ (al-Hakim, al-Mustadrak; al-Tabarani)

⚔️

حَیْدَرِ کَرَّار

Haidar-e-Karrar

Hamla Aawar Sher

Maani wa Ahmiyat

Haidar ka matlab "sher" aur Karrar ka matlab "baar baar hamla karne waala" hai. Yeh laqab Ali ke munfarid jangi andaaz-e-ladaai ki taazeem karta hai — musalsal, be-khauf aur hareefon ke liye naaqaabil-e-bardaasht.

Jabke bohat se jangju mukhtasar waqt ke liye bahaaduri dikhaa sakte hain, Ali musalsal jaarihana ladaai ke liye mashoor the. Jang-e-Khaybar mein aap ne is qadar shiddat se ladaai ki ke kai talwaarein toot gayin jab tak Nabi ﷺ ne aap ko Zulfiqar ata nahi farmaya. Aap ne akele qile par hamla kiya, uska darwaaza ukhaadha aur dhaal ke taur par istimaal kiya — aisa kaarnama jise baad mein kai aadmiyon ne dohraane ki koshish ki lekin darwaaza uthha bhi na sake.

"Ali ne akele Jang-e-Badr mein dushman ki aadhi fauj ko halaak kiya."

— Jang-e-Badr (624 CE) ki taarikhi riwaayaat

👑

أَمِيرُ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ

Ameer-ul-Momineen

Mominon Ke Sardaar

Maani wa Ahmiyat

Ameer-ul-Momineen Muslim Ummat ke sarbaraah ko diya jaane waala sarkaari laqab hai. Chauthe Khalifa-e-Raashid (656–661 CE) ke taur par Ali ne yeh laqab adl ke aise azm ke saath barqaraar rakha jo aaj bhi misaali hai.

Aap se pehle ya baad ke bohat se hukumraanon ke bar-aks, Ali ne iqtidaar mein rehte huye intehaayi zaati saadgi ikhtiyaar ki. Aap khud apne kapde seete, saadha khaana khaate, Bait-ul-Maal se apne hisse se ek dirham zyada lene se inkaar karte, aur shaksi taur par ghareebon ki khabar-geeri ke liye galiyon mein nikalte — ba-shamool apni hukumat ke ghair-Muslim shehrion ki.

"Tumhari raaya do tarah ki hai: ya toh woh emaan mein tumhare bhai hain ya insaaniyat mein tumhare baraabar."

— Hazrat Ali (RA), Malik al-Ashtar ko khat, Nahj al-Balagha

🌟

اَلْمُرْتَضٰی

Al-Murtaza

Pasandeeda / Jin Se Khuda Raazi Hai

Maani wa Ahmiyat

Al-Murtaza "riza" se maakhooz hai jis ka matlab "khushnodi" ya "manzoori" hai. Yeh bataata hai ke Allah Ali se raazi hai aur Ali woh hain jinhein Allah ne chuna aur pasand farmaya. Yeh laqab un par ata karda ilahi qabooliyat aur izzat ki akkaasi karta hai.

Aap ka poora naam aksar Ali al-Murtaza — Ali jin se Khuda raazi hai — bayaan kiya jaata hai. Yeh laqab aap ki mukammal aqeedat, qurbaani aur har haal mein Khuda ki itaa'at ki zindagi ka khulaasa hai — jawaani se shahaadat tak.

🤲

أَبُو تُرَاب

Abu Turab

Mitti Ke Waalid

Maani wa Ahmiyat

Yeh pyaara laqab Ali ko Nabi Kareem ﷺ ne khud ata farmaya. Ek din jab Nabi ﷺ ne Ali ko masjid mein sote huye paaya aur un ke jism par mitti lagi hui thi, aap ﷺ ne pyaar se mitti saaf ki aur unhein "Abu Turab" — Mitti Ke Waalid — pukaara.

Yeh kisi tanqeed se door, Ali ke sab se pasandeeda alqaabaat mein se ek ban gaya. Yeh un ki gehri aajzi aur duniya se be-raghbati ki alamat hai. Jabke aap Khalifa aur Nabi ﷺ ke chacha zaad bhai ki haisiyat se aish-o-aaraam mein reh sakte the, aap ne saadha zindagi ka intikhaab kiya — zameen par sona, pewand lage kapde pehenna, aur zameen ki mitti ki tarah mutawaaze rehna.

"Ali ko Abu Turab ka laqab kisi bhi aur naam se zyada pasand tha aur jab unhein is naam se pukaara jaata toh khush hote the."

— Sahih al-Bukhari

💫

وَلِيُّ اللّٰہ

Wali Allah

Khuda Ke Dost / Khuda Ke Naayib

Maani wa Ahmiyat

Arabi mein Wali ke mutaaddid maani hain — dost, muhaafiz, sarparast aur aaqa. Wali Allah Ali ko Khuda ke qareebi dost aur mahboob ke taur par naamzad karta hai. Quran Majeed Aayat-e-Wilayat (5:55) mein is tasawwur ka zikr karta hai.

Sufi riwaayaat mein Ali ko wilayat (tasawwuf) ka sarchashma samjha jaata hai aur taqreeban har Sufi silsila (tareeqat) apni roohaani zanjeer (silsila) ko un se jodta hai. Unhein Nabi ﷺ ka roohaani waaris samjha jaata hai — woh jin ke zariye Islam ki baatini, roohaani jihat aane waali naslon tak pahunchi.

"Tumhara Wali (dost/sarparast) sirf Allah hai, Uska Rasool hai, aur woh momineen hain jo namaaz qaayim karte aur ruku ki haalat mein zakat dete hain."

— Quran 5:55 (bohat se ulama ka khayaal hai ke yeh aayat Ali ke baare mein naazil hui)

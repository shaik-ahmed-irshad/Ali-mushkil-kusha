Jangein wa Fauji Miraas

Islam ka naaqaabil-e-shikast jangju — Badr ki pehli jang se Khaybar ke qile tak, Hazrat Ali (RA) ne kabhi shikast ka zaaiqha nahi chakha

⚔️ Zulfiqar — Afsaanwi Talwaar

لَا فَتَىٰ إِلَّا عَلِيٌّ لَا سَيْفَ إِلَّا ذُو الْفِقَار

"Koi bahaadur jawaan nahi siwaaye Ali ke, aur koi talwaar nahi siwaaye Zulfiqar ke."

Zulfiqar woh afsaanwi do dhaari talwaar thi jo Hazrat Ali (RA) ko Nabi Kareem ﷺ ne Jang-e-Uhud ke dauraan ata farmaai. Yeh Islami taarikh ki sab se mashoor talwaar aur ilahi adl wa taaqat ki alaamat ban gayi. Is ke naam ka matlab "Reedh Ki Haddi Waali" ya "Mehron Ko Cheerne Waali" hai.

624 CE

Jang-e-Badr

🏴

Islami taarikh ki pehli badi fauji mudbheed. 313 Musalmaanon ne taqreeban 1,000 musalah Qureshi jangjuon ka saamna kiya. Ali sirf taqreeban 23 saal ke the, alambardar the aur jang ke aaghaz mein mubaarazat ke liye chune gaye teen soormaon mein se ek the.

Ali ne ibtidaai mubaarazat mein Walid bin Utba ko shikast di, phir Hamza aur Ubayda ki madad ki. Asl jang ke dauraan Ali ne ghair-maamooli bahaaduri se ladaai ki aur dushman ki halaakaton ka ek bada hissa un ke haathon hua.

"Badr mein dushman ki taqreeban aadhi halaakaten Ali ke haathon huin."

625 CE

Jang-e-Uhud

⛰️

Uhud mein jab Khalid bin Walid ke shehswaaron ke peechhe se achaanak hamle ki wajah se bohat se Sahaba bhaag gaye, Ali Nabi ﷺ ke saath datte rahe. Aap ne Nabi ﷺ ko apne jism se dhaaltay huye 16 se zyada zakhm khaaye.

Jab yeh afwaah phaili ke Nabi ﷺ shaheed ho gaye hain, Ali un chand logon mein se the jo datte rahe, hamle par hamle ko rokte rahe. Isi jang mein aasmaan se aawaaz sunaai di: "Koi bahaadur jawaan nahi siwaaye Ali ke, aur koi talwaar nahi siwaaye Zulfiqar ke."

Ali ne Nabi ﷺ ki hifaazat karte huye 16 zakhm khaaye aur ek qadam bhi peechhe nahi hate.

627 CE

Jang-e-Khandaq (Ahzaab)

🏰

Jab 10,000 dushman faujion ne Madina ka muhaasira kiya, Musalmaanon ne Salman Farsi ke mashware par khandaq khodi. Sab se naazuk lamha us waqt aaya jab mashoor Arab jangju Amr ibn Abd Wudd — jise hazaar jangjuon ke baraabar samjha jaata tha — ne khandaq uboor kar ke Musalmaanon ko mubaarazat ka challenge diya.

Ali ke siwa kisi ne un ka saamna karne ki himmat nahi ki. Nabi ﷺ ne farmaaya: "Poora emaan poore kufr ke muqaable mein nikla hai." Ali ne Amr se shadeed mubaarazat ki aur use qatl kiya, jis se dushman ka hausla toot gaya aur woh aakhirkaar paspa ho gaye.

"Khandaq ke din Ali ka ek waar tamaam jinnon aur insaanon ki ibaadat se behtar hai."

— Nabi Kareem ﷺ se mansoob

628 CE

Jang-e-Khaybar

🏯

Khaybar ke mazboot qile ka muhaasira Ali ke fauji career ki sab se draamaai waaqiaat mein se ek tha. Jab dusre commander qile ko fatah karne mein naakaam rahe toh Nabi ﷺ ne farmaaya:

"Kal main jhanda us shakhs ko dunga jo Allah aur Uske Rasool se mohabbat karta hai, aur jis se Allah aur Uska Rasool mohabbat karte hain. Woh waapas nahi aayega jab tak Allah use fatah ata na kar de."

— Nabi Kareem ﷺ (Sahih al-Bukhari wa Sahih Muslim)

Agli subah Nabi ﷺ ne jhanda Ali ko diya jo aankhon ke infection mein mubtala the. Nabi ﷺ ne apna mubaarak luaab un ki aankhon par lagaaya aur fori shifa ho gayi. Phir Ali ne qile ki taraf march kiya, soorma Marhab se mubaarazat ki aur use qatl kiya, phir Khaybar ka azeem lohe ka darwaaza ukhaadha aur dhaal ke taur par istimaal kiya.

Baad mein logon ke ek giroah ne darwaaze ko ulattne ki koshish ki — aath ya is se zyada aadmiyon ne koshish ki lekin phir bhi sahih tarah se uttha na sake. Yeh waaqia Ali ko ata karda ilahi taaqat ka afsaanwi saboot ban gaya.

630 CE

Jang-e-Hunayn

🏹

Fatah-e-Makkah ke baad 12,000 Muslim fauji Hunayn mein Hawazin qabeele ke khilaaf nikle. Jab dushman ne ek tang waadi mein un par ghaat laga kar hamla kiya toh badi Muslim fauj mein khauf phail gaya aur bohat se bhaag nikle. Ali un chand logon mein se the jo Nabi ﷺ ke ird-gird datte rahe aur us waqt tak ladte rahe jab tak Musalman dobaara jama ho kar fatah haasil na kar li.

Hunayn mein un ki saabit-qadmi ne, jaisa ke Uhud mein, saabit kiya ke un ki bahaaduri mahaz jaar-hiyat nahi balke usooli bahaaduri thi — woh wahaan datte rahe jahaan sab se zyada zaroorat thi, khawah mushkilaat kuch bhi hon.

Fauji Akhlaqiyaat wa Miraas

Ali ko dusre jangjuon se jo cheez mumtaaz karti thi woh sirf un ki be-misaal ladaai ki salaahiyat nahi balke jang mein un ka ghair-maamooli akhlaqi zaabita tha:

  • Kabhi pehle waar nahi kiya — hamesha hareef ke hamle ka intezaar karte
  • Kabhi bhaagge dushman ko nahi maara — ise be-izzati samajhte the
  • Kabhi murdon ki be-hurmati nahi ki — gire huye hareefon ke saath izzat se pesh aate
  • Amr ibn Abd Wudd ko lamha bhar ke liye chhoda jab Amr ne un par thooka — ghussay mein nahi balke sirf Allah ke liye qatl ka intekhaab kiya
  • Aurton, bachon aur aam shehrion ki hifaazat har tanaazu mein
  • Kabhi zaati faaide ke liye nahi lade — un ka maqsad sirf emaan aur adl ka difaa tha

In usoolon ne unhein na sirf apne daur ka sab se azeem jangju banaaya balke jaaiz jang ka aisa namoona banaaya jise aaj bhi fauji akhlaqiyaat ke maahireen zair-e-muta'ala rakhte hain.

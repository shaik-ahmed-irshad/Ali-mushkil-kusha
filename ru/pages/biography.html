زندگی نامہ

Hazrat Ali (RA) Ki Hayat-e-Mubaraka

Khana Ka'ba ke andar mo'jizati wilaadat se Masjid-e-Kufa mein shahadat tak — Ameer-ul-Momineen ki ghair-mamooli zindagi

1 Wilaadat Aur Ibtidai Zindagi

وِلَادَتِ مُبَارَک

Hazrat Ali Ibn Abi Talib (Razi Allahu Anhu) 13 Rajab, taqreeban 600 Isvi (baaz riwaayaat ke mutabiq 601 Isvi) mein Makkah Mukarramah mein paida hue. Unki wilaadat ko jo cheez ghair-mamooli banati hai woh yeh wasi paimaane par bayaan ki jaane wali riwaayat hai ke woh Khana Ka'ba ke andar — Islam ki sab se muqaddas jagah — paida hue, jo unhe taarikh mein Baitullah ke andar paida hone wali wahid shakhsiyat banati hai.

Unke waalid Abu Talib Ibn Abdul Muttalib Banu Hashim qabeele ke sardaar aur Nabi Muhammad ﷺ ke chacha aur muhafiz thay. Unki waalida Fatima Bint Asad ibtidai Musalmano mein se theen aur Nabi ﷺ unhe apni maa ka darja dete thay.

Bahut chhoti umar se hi Hazrat Ali ki parwarish Nabi Muhammad ﷺ ke ghar mein hui. Jab Makkah mein shadeed qehat padi to Nabi ﷺ ne Ali ko apni kafaalat mein le liya taake Abu Talib ke bade khandaan ka bojh halka ho. Mustaqbil ke Nabi ki barah-e-raast nigranee mein yeh parwarish Ali ko us ghair-mamooli shakhsiyat mein dhaalne ka sabab bani.

"Mujhe Nabi ﷺ ki god mein paala gaya. Woh mujhe apne haathon se khilaate aur mere liye khaana chabaate thay. Unhon ne mujhe kabhi jhooth bolte ya dhoka dete nahin paaya."

— Hazrat Ali (RA), Nahj al-Balagha, Khutba 192

2 Qubool-e-Islam

قبولِ اسلام

Jab Nabi Muhammad ﷺ ko pehli wahi mili aur unhon ne logon ko khufia taur par Islam ki dawat dena shuru ki to Ali sab se pehle qubool karne walon mein shaamil thay. Unki umar ke baare mein riwaayaat mukhtalif hain — taqreeban 10 se 13 saal — lekin is par ittifaaq hai ke woh pehle mard aur pehle naujawaan thay jinhon ne Islam qubool kiya.

Hazrat Ali ne apni zindagi mein kabhi kisi but ki pooja nahin ki. Nabi ﷺ ke ghar mein palne ki wajah se unka emaan fori aur mukammal tha. Unhon ne Makkah mein ibtidai Musalmano par hone wale har masaaib aur eeza rasaani mein Nabi ﷺ ka saath diya.

Nabi ﷺ ne baad mein Ali ko "Apne Haroon" kaha — jo sirf qurbat nahin balke be-misaal roohani aur qiyadat ke rishte ki nishandahi karta hai.

3 Laylat al-Mabit — Qurbani Ki Raat

لیلۃ المبیت

Jab Quraish ne Nabi Muhammad ﷺ ko unke bistar par qatl karne ki saazish rachi to Nabi ﷺ ko Allah Ta'ala ka hukm mila ke Madinah hijrat karein. Unki mehfooz rawaangi ke liye kisi ko bataur na'am al-badal unke bistar par sona zaroori tha.

Ali ne bila ta'ammul razaakaraana taur par pesh hue. Yeh jaante hue ke qaatilon ne nangi talwaaron ke saath ghar ko ghera hua hai, naujawaan Ali Nabi ﷺ ke bistar par let gaye aur Nabi ﷺ ki sabz chaadar odh li. Unhon ne poori raat sukoon se aaraam kiya jabke qaatil baahar fajr ka intizaar kar rahe thay.

Jab qaatil fajr mein andar daakhil hue to unhon ne Ali ko paaya. Apni jaan qurbaan karne ki yeh aamadagi Islami riwaayat mein yaad ki jaati hai. Bahut se ulama ka maanna hai ke Quran ki Ayat 2:207 isi waaqiye ki taraf ishaara karti hai:

"Aur logon mein se woh bhi hai jo Allah ki raza haasil karne ke liye apni jaan bech deta hai. Aur Allah apne bandon par bahut meharbaan hai."

— Quran 2:207

4 Hazrat Fatima al-Zahra (RA) Se Shaadi

شادی حضرت فاطمہ الزہراء سے

Taqreeban 623 Isvi (2 Hijri) mein Ali ne Fatima al-Zahra (Razi Allahu Anha) se shaadi ki jo Nabi Muhammad ﷺ ki sab se mehboob sahebzadi theen. Bahut se mumtaaz Sahaba ne unka rishta maanga tha, lekin Nabi ﷺ ne sab ko inkaar kar diya — woh Ali ka intizaar kar rahe thay, jinhe Allah ne unke liye chuna tha.

Unki shaadi, agarche dunyawi lihaaz se saada thi, Islami taarikh ki sab se azeem shaadion mein ek ban gayi. Un se woh aulaad hui jo Islami taarikh ki markazi shakhsiyaat bane: Imam Hasan, Imam Husain, Zainab, aur Umm Kulthum.

Nabi ﷺ ne farmaya: "Fatima Jannat ki khawateen ki sardaar hain, aur Hasan aur Husain Jannat ke naujawanon ke sardaar hain." Ali aur Fatima ka gharaana Ahl-e-Bait (Nabi ﷺ ka khandaan) ke qalb ke taur par mo'azzaz hai.

5 Jangi Bahaduri — Naaqabil-e-Shikast Mujahid

جنگی بہادری — ناقابلِ شکست مجاہد

Hazrat Ali ki jangi maharat afsanawi hai. Unhon ne Nabi ﷺ ki zindagi mein taqreeban har badi jang mein shirkat ki aur zaati jang mein kabhi shikast nahin khai. Woh mashhoor do dhaari talwaar Zulfiqar rakhte thay jo Nabi ﷺ ne unhe ata farmai.

Aham Jangein:

  • Ghazwa Badr (624 Isvi) — Ali ne muta'addid mumtaaz Quraishi jangjuon ko shikast di, jin mein Waleed Ibn Utba shaamil thay, Islam ki pehli badi fauji fatah mein faisla-kun kirdaar ada kiya.
  • Ghazwa Uhud (625 Isvi) — Jab bahut se log maidan-e-jang se bhaag gaye to Ali Nabi ﷺ ke difaa mein saabit-qadam rahe, muta'addid zakhm khaye lekin kabhi peeche nahin hate.
  • Ghazwa Khandaq (627 Isvi) — Ali ne khaufnaak Amr Ibn Abd al-Wud ko ek ba-muqabla ek jang mein shikast di, aisa lamha jis ke baare mein Nabi ﷺ ne farmaya "poori insaaniyat ki ibaadat se badh kar hai."
  • Ghazwa Khaybar (628 Isvi) — Jab doosre kai dinon tak nakaam rahe to Nabi ﷺ ne farmaya: "Kal main jhanda us shakhs ko doonga jo Allah aur Us ke Rasool se mohabbat karta hai aur Allah aur Uska Rasool us se mohabbat karte hain." Unhon ne Ali ko diya, jinhon ne qila fatah kiya aur ek haath se Khaybar ka darwaza uthaya.
  • Fatah Makkah (630 Isvi) — Ali ne Ka'ba mein 360 buton ko todne mein madad ki aur use Ek Khuda ki ibaadat ke liye bahaal kiya.

"Ali jaisa koi jawaan nahin aur Zulfiqar jaisi koi talwaar nahin."

— لَا فَتَىٰ إِلَّا عَلِيٌّ لَا سَيْفَ إِلَّا ذُو الْفِقَار

6 Khilafat (656–661 Isvi)

خلافت

656 Isvi mein Khalifa Soem Hazrat Usman ki shahadat ke baad, Ali ko chauthe Khalifa Rashid ke taur par muntakhab kiya gaya. Unki khilafat, agarche siyaasi halchal se nishaanzad rahi jab woh adl aur islahaat ki koshish kar rahe thay, usool hukumrani ka namoona hai.

Unhon ne daar-ul-hukumat Madinah se Kufa (maujuda Iraq) muntaqil kiya aur wasi paimaane par islahaat naafiz keen:

  • Baitul Maal ki tamaam shehriyon mein baraabar taqseem, bila tafreeq martaba
  • Badunwaan hukkaam ki bartarfi aur ehtisaab ki bahali
  • Aqliyaton aur ghair Muslim shehriyon ke huqooq ka tahaffuz
  • Zaati saadgi — woh apni ra'aya ke ghareeb tareen fard ki tarah rehte thay

Unki khilafat ko teen bade androoni tanaazaat ka saamna raha: Jang-e-Jamal (656 Isvi), Jang-e-Siffin (657 Isvi), aur Jang-e-Nahrawan (658 Isvi). In challenges ke bawajood unhon ne kabhi adl se samjhauta nahin kiya.

Misr ke liye muqarrar karda governor Maalik Ashtar ko unka mashhoor khat taarikh-e-insaaniyat mein hukumrani ke behtareen dastawezaat mein shumaar kiya jaata hai. Aqwam-e-Muttahida ne ise aadilana hukumrani ki misaal ke taur par hawala diya hai.

7 Shahadat Aur Virasat

شہادت و وراثت

19 Ramzan 40 Hijri (661 Isvi) ko, jab woh Masjid-e-Kufa mein Namaz-e-Fajr padha rahe thay, Khawaarij ke ek fard Ibn Muljam ne zahar-aalood talwaar se unke sar par waar kiya.

Mehlak zakhm ke bawajood Ali ke pehle alfaaz thay "Ka'ba ke Rab ki qasam, main kaamyaab ho gaya!" (فُزْتُ وَرَبِّ الْكَعْبَة). Woh do din tak zinda rahe, is dauran unhon ne apne beton aur Muslim ummat ko aakhri naseehat di — hatta ke apne qaatil ke saath bhi munsifaana sulook ka hukm diya aur kaha ke use khaana paani diya jaye.

Woh 21 Ramzan 40 Hijri (28 January 661 Isvi) ko taqreeban 63 saal ki umar mein wisaal farma gaye. Unhe khufia taur par Najaf (maujuda Iraq) mein dafnaya gaya jahan aaj Rauza-e-Imam Ali qaim hai — duniya ki sab se zyada ziyarat ki jaane wali jagahon mein se ek.

"Ka'ba ke Rab ki qasam, main kaamyaab ho gaya!" — Zarb lagne par unke aakhri alfaaz, jo is baat ka izhaar thay ke Khuda ki raah mein shahadat unki haqeeqi kaamyabi thi.

— فُزْتُ وَرَبِّ الْكَعْبَة

Unki miras tamaam Islami masaalik mein zinda hai. Ahl-e-Sunnat ke nazdeeq woh chauthe Khalifa hain, Ahl-e-Tashayyu ke nazdeeq pehle Imam, Sufia ke liye aham roohani shakhsiyat, aur tamaam insaniyat ke liye adl, ilm aur bahaduri ki alamat. 1,400 saal se zaaid arse se ulama, fuqaha, jangjoo, shuara aur haq ke mutalaashi Ali se rahnumai haasil karte aa rahe hain.

Hayat-e-Mubaraka Ki Timeline

~600 Isvi

Ka'ba Mein Wilaadat

13 Rajab ko Makkah mein Khana Ka'ba ke andar paida hue — yeh i'zaaz kisi aur ko nahin mila.

~610 Isvi

Pehle Mard Musalmaan

Taqreeban 10 saal ki umar mein Ali pehle mard aur pehle naujawaan bane jinhon ne Islam qubool kiya.

622 Isvi

Laylat al-Mabit

Hijrat ki raat Nabi ﷺ ke bistar par so kar apni jaan khatre mein daali.

623 Isvi

Fatima (RA) Se Shaadi

Nabi Muhammad ﷺ ki mehboob sahebzadi Fatima al-Zahra se shaadi.

624 Isvi

Ghazwa Badr

Islam ki pehli badi jang mein bahaduri se lade, mumtaaz Quraishi jangjuon ko shikast di.

628 Isvi

Ghazwa Khaybar

Khaybar ka qila fatah kiya aur uska bhaari darwaza uthaya — afsanawi taaqat ka muzahira.

632 Isvi

Waaqia Ghadeer Khum

Nabi ﷺ ne farmaya: "Jis ka main maula hoon, Ali uske maula hain" — Islami taarikh ka aham tareen lamha.

656 Isvi

Chauthe Khalifa

Khalifa Usman ki shahadat ke baad chauthe Khalifa Rashid bane.

661 Isvi

Kufa Mein Shahadat

19 Ramzan ko Namaz-e-Fajr ke dauraan zarb lagi. 21 Ramzan ko shaheed hue. Najaf, Iraq mein madfoon.

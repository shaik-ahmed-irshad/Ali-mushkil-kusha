مَعْرَضُ الفُنُون

Gallery & Islamic Art

Islamic calligraphy, geometric art, and visual tributes honoring Hazrat Ali (RA)

In Islamic tradition, the beauty of the divine is expressed not through figurative representation but through the sacred art of calligraphy, geometric patterns, and architectural ornamentation. Below is a curated collection of calligraphic compositions celebrating the names, titles, and wisdom of Hazrat Ali (RA).

عَلِیٌّ
مُشْکِلْ کُشَا
Thuluth Script

Ali Mushkil Kusha

"Ali, the Solver of Difficulties" — the primordial invocation of those seeking the intercession and spiritual aid of Hazrat Ali.

بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ
ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
Naskh Script

Bismillah ir-Rahman ir-Rahim

"In the name of God, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful" — every recitation, action, and endeavor begins with these words.

أَسَدُ ٱللَّهِ
ٱلْغَالِب
Diwani Script

Asadullahil Ghalib

"The Ever-Victorious Lion of God" — the title bestowed upon Ali for his unmatched bravery in battle.

نَادِ عَلِيًّا
مَظْهَرَ الْعَجَائِبِ
Thuluth Script

Nad-e-Ali

"Call upon Ali, the manifestation of wonders" — the opening line of one of the most recited supplications in the Muslim world.

لَا فَتَىٰ
إِلَّا عَلِيّ
Kufic Script

La Fata illa Ali

"There is no youth (brave one) except Ali" — the angelic proclamation heard at the Battle of Uhud celebrating Ali's valor.

أَمِيرُ
ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ
Nastaliq Script

Amir al-Mu'minin

"Commander of the Faithful" — the title of Ali as the fourth Rightly Guided Caliph of Islam.

اَنَا مَدِينَةُ الْعِلْمِ
وَعَلِيٌّ بَابُهَا
Thuluth Script

City of Knowledge

"I am the city of knowledge and Ali is its gate" — Hadith of the Prophet (PBUH) affirming Ali's unrivaled mastery of Islamic knowledge.

مَنْ كُنْتُ مَوْلَاهُ
فَهَذَا عَلِيٌّ مَوْلَاهُ
Naskh Script

Declaration of Ghadir

"Whoever I am his master, Ali is his master" — the historic proclamation at Ghadir Khumm.

ذُو ٱلْفِقَار
Kufic Script

Zulfiqar — The Legendary Sword

The double-bladed sword gifted to Ali by the Prophet (PBUH), Zulfiqar became a symbol of divine justice and the defense of truth.

إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ جَمِيلٌ يُحِبُّ ٱلْجَمَالَ

"Indeed, God is beautiful and loves beauty." — Hadith

Islamic calligraphy is considered one of the highest forms of art, transforming the written word into visual prayer.
